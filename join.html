Join

To join, players need to become members of

  1. UK Ultimate.
  2. Tonbridge Ultimate.
To become a member of UK Ultimate click here. Basic Membership should suffice for most players. For 2025/26 this is £11.00. Once you have become a member of Flow part of your membership fee will cover this renewal. If you are unsure what membership level of UK Ultimate you will need please speak with a member of the club. Please note your UK Ultimate Membership Number.

To Become a member of Tonbridge Ultimate complete EITHER of the following player Questionnaires

  • Under 18 Membership.
  • Over 18 Membership
And pay the Club Membership fee for 2025/6. All new members receive a new “Flow” branded Ultrastar 175 Disc

Membership fees (and perks) will depend upon your age. All returning players will have basic membership of the UKU covered as part of the club membership fee.
Juniors (u14/u17 and u20): Get two tournament entry fees if joining before 31 January 2026.

  1. Returning Juniors - £60
  2. New Juniors 1 Sept 2025 - 31 Jan 2026 - £50 + UKU Membership
  3. New Juniors 1 Feb 2026 - 21 July 2026 - £30 + UKU Membership but only one tournemnt entry fee is covered.
  4. New Juniors 22 July 2026 - 31 Aug 2026 - £0 + UKU Membership. No free disc included but these may be purchased at cost.
Seniors: No tournament entry fees covered these will be paid on an as and when basis.
  1. Returning Seniors - £40
  2. New Seniors - £40 + UKU Membership

All club communication is currently channelled through our Tonbridge Ultimate WhatsApp Community. Contact the club using the form below to receive an invite. Banking details for club membership payments are in the WhatsApp Community Header.
