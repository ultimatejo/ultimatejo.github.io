Join

To JOIN, players need to become members of

  1. UK Ultimate.
  2. Tonbridge Ultimate.
To become a member of UK Ultimate click here. Basic Membership should suffice for most players. For 2024/25 this is £10.50 If you are unsure what membership level of UK Ultimate you will need please speak with a member of the club. Please note your UK Ultimate Membership Number.

To Become a member of Tonbridge Ultimate complete EITHER of the following player Questionnaires

  • Under 18 Membership.
  • Over 18 Membership
And pay the Club Membership fee for 2024/5 of £30 for over 18s and £20 for under 18s. All new members receive a new “Flow” branded Ultrastar 175 Disc

All club communication is currently channelled through our Tonbridge Ultimate WhatsApp Community. Contact the club using the form below to receive an invite. Banking details for club membership payments are in the WhatsApp Community Header.
