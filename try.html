Try Us Out

We offer three weeks of free training to all new players whether they are 9 or 60 years old.
New junior players are welcome. Our Junior section is growing significantly with Ultimate having been introduced at several local schools. Most of our juniors are in years 7-11. We prefer but donâ€™t mandate parents to briefly introduce themselves to one of the coaching team at an U18s first attendance.
We are a family friendly club so parents of prospective junior players are also welcome to come and learn some of the skills their children are developing. We have a number of parents who are attend along with their children. A parents guide is available at UK Ultimate
When you attend for the first time
  • Bring a Dark and a Light Top.
  • Bring refreshments/water or cash to buy refreshments from the mobile coffee van.
  • Sunblock or Waterproofs (depending on the weather.
  • Appropriate footware (football/hockey boots).
  • Layers, if its cold.
After your 3 weeks we require players to become members of the club and National Governing Body. This is inexpensive. To become a member click Join.
